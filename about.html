Our Journey

Founded in 2023, KayGee bakes started of as a plan of making a living, which turned out to be a dream of serving tastefull treats in a welcoming manner. over the years due to this dream, the business started diversifying and offering more than it started with.

Our founder, Kgomotso kgarebe, started baking as a way to keep up with certain needs, she started way before she thought about making it into a business with a digial presence, she was just selling to a few people in her circle in her home kitchen. Her skills and creativity picked up momentum. Today, we continue to uphold her commitment to excellence in every bite.

Our Values

At KayGee bakes, we believe in connections made through a selection of taste, whether is through family gatherings or an individual bringing something from the bakery to an outside function. At KayGee Bakes the products are not only created to archieve a significant taste but also to insert a certain feeling. Our values include:

  • Quality Ingredients: We only use the best ingredients, sourced from well known brands to keep the taste fresh.
  • Creativity: Our believe in inovation is the key to evreything flavourfull and everything creatively designed.
  • Community: We are proud to be part of the Protea glen community meaning making it easire to personalise every interaction and offering. Also giving back to those who assist.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices, from packaging to sourcing ingredients.
